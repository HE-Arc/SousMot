Rules

Because is always better to know what you're doing !

SousMot is a multiplayer game based on Wordle, a game where you have to guess a word by successive
attempts, as well as the TV game Motus, a game in which two teams competed in order to guess the
most words possible.


The Game

In the game view, try to find a word that is in the dictionary and fits in one row of the table.
When you find it, click enter button and the letters that you have entered will color depending on the position

Time Attack

Each player has a set time to find as many words as possible. The player who finds the most words wins.
