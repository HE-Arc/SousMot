Rules

Because it's always better to know what you're doing !

SousMot is a multiplayer game based on Wordle, a game where you have to guess a word by successive attempts, as well as the French TV game Motus, the french adaption of Lingo.

How to play

In the game view, try to find a word that is in the dictionary and fits in one row of the table. You can use the keyboard at the bottom of the page or just write it ! Then click enter and the letters that you have entered will color depending their position.

Time Attack

Each player has a set time to find as many words as possible. The player who finds the most words wins.
